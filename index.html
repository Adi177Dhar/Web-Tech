What is Gateways?

Gateways are an annually conducted fest by the Computer science department of Christ University.

This year will be the second Gateways fest being conducted online, due to COVID-19.

How to register?

Click on the register button. It will take you to a form, where you can fill up your details.

Registration is open till 30th October 2021, so fill up the form if you wish to be a part of Gateways.
